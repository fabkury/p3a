# p3a-gotchi: Pixel Art Sprite Pack Landscape

Art-only survey compiled March 2026. Evaluates free and paid pixel art sprite packs for use in a virtual pet feature on p3a's 720x720 IPS display with nearest-neighbor upscaling.

---

## The Market Gap

After surveying 40+ packs across itch.io, OpenGameArt, Unity Asset Store, GameDevMarket, CraftPix, and Kenney: **no single pack combines rich evolution stages + many emotional animation states + pixel art style + permissive license.** The packs that exist fall into three camps:

1. **Pet-specific states but few creatures**: G.Pessoa, ToffeeCraft, VectoRaith
2. **Many creatures and animations but platformer-oriented**: SeethingSwarm, Calciumtrice
3. **Deep evolution systems but static (no animation)**: Akoro, WorthyPie

Combining packs or commissioning custom art is the realistic path to a complete tamagotchi art kit.
