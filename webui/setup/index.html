Wi-Fi setup

Connect to Wi-Fi

Device names are useful only if you have more than one p3a. If you have just one, it's more convenient to leave it empty.

This name will become part of the device's LAN host name. Lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only.

After saving, p3a will reboot and join your Wi-Fi network. Then open http://p3a.local/ to control your p3a.
